Materials:

	- Blender;

Ingredients:
	- 200 ml of milk or 200 ;
	- 1 apple;
	- 1 banana;
	- 2-3 strawberries;

Directions:

	1. Put all the ingredients into the blender and mix for 30 seconds.

	2. If there are still big pieces which are not cut put the medium into the blender again and mix for another 30 seconds.

	3. If there are still big pieces then leave them since the blender cannot cut them smaller.
